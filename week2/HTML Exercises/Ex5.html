How to boil rice...

  1. Get pot, or saucepan.
  2. Measure out the rice and add into the pot.
  3. Wash the rice with water until the water in the pot is clear. Pour out remaining water.
  4. Measure out the water and add it to the pot with the rice.
  5. Bring the water/rice to boil.
  6. Then, leave covered on a low heat until there is no more water left at the bottom of the rice.
  7. Once the rice is cooked, fluff the rice with a fork, serve and enjoy.
